Agric Economics App

Welcome to the Agric Economics App! This tool is designed to assist farmers and agricultural proffessionals in managing their crop data and perform economic analysis with ease. By entering your crop details, you can get valuable insights into the costs, yiels and potential profits of your farming operations

Features

  • Data Entry:Easily input your crop data including crop name, area, cost, projected yield and market price
  • Economic Analysis:Get detailed economic analysis to understand your potential revenue and profit.
  • User-Friendly Interface:Simple and intuitive interface to make data entry and analysis quick and easy.
  • Secure Data StorageYour data is securely stored and managed.

How to Use

To get started fill the form below with your crop information. Once submitted, the app will provide you with a detailed analysis of your crop's economic performance. You can view the analysis in the Economic Analysis section
